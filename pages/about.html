About SmartQuiz

SmartQuiz is an engaging offline quiz app designed to help users test and improve their knowledge across various subjects and categories. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just curious, SmartQuiz offers a fun and rewarding way to challenge your brain.

Key Features

  • Play quizzes offline anytime, anywhere
  • Two main categories: Educational and General
  • Multiple subjects with 12 levels per subject
  • 20 multiple-choice questions per level with a 5-minute timer
  • Instant feedback on incorrect answers
  • Leaderboard and medal system to track your progress
  • Profile previews, avatar customization, and in-app shop (using earned coins)

Development

SmartQuiz was originally released on the Google Play Store on March 25, 2022. After a period of inactivity, development was resumed seriously in May 2025. The app is fully built by a single developer.

Monetization

SmartQuiz is completely free to use and does not include any real-money purchases. Revenue is generated through AdMob ads. All in-app currency (coins) is earned by playing quizzes.
